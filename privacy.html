Privacy Policy - X Auto Engage

Last updated: December 12, 2025

1. Introduction

X Auto Engage ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Authentication Information

  • What we collect: Your OpenAI or Groq API keys
  • Why we collect it: To enable AI-powered reply generation features
  • How we store it: Stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage API
  • Who has access: Only you. We never transmit API keys to our servers

2.2 User Activity Data

  • What we collect: Engagement statistics including:
    • Number of likes, replies, and retweets performed
    • Timestamps of engagement activities
    • Automation session history
  • Why we collect it: To track your engagement progress and provide analytics
  • How we store it: Stored locally in your browser
  • Who has access: Only you. This data never leaves your device

2.3 Website Content

  • What we collect: Text content from X/Twitter posts you engage with
  • Why we collect it: To generate contextual AI-powered replies
  • How we process it: Sent to OpenAI or Groq API (based on your selection) for AI processing
  • Storage: Processed temporarily, not permanently stored by the Extension
Important: The Extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit:
  • Your X/Twitter password or login credentials
  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Financial information
  • Browsing history outside of X/Twitter

3. Third-Party Services

This Extension uses third-party AI services to generate replies:

  • OpenAI API: Privacy Policy
  • Groq API: Privacy Policy

When you use AI reply features, tweet content is sent to your chosen AI provider. Please review their privacy policies to understand how they handle data.

4. Data Security

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • API keys are stored securely in Chrome's local storage
  • No data is sent to our servers or any third party except the AI provider you choose
  • All engagement statistics remain on your device

5. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all stored data through the Extension settings
  • Delete: Clear all data by:
    • Using the "Clear Data" option in Extension settings
    • Uninstalling the Extension (removes all stored data)
  • Stop Collection: Disable or pause the Extension at any time
  • Export: Contact us to request a copy of your data

6. Data Retention

  • All data is stored locally and retained until you delete it or uninstall the Extension
  • Tweet content sent to AI providers is processed according to their retention policies
  • We do not maintain any server-side copies of your data

7. Children's Privacy

This Extension is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
